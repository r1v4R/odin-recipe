DOSA RECIPE !

DESCRIPTION

Dosa is a thin, crispy, and golden-brown pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. It is a versatile South Indian dish that can be enjoyed plain or filled with a spiced potato mixture, served alongside chutney and sambar.

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
